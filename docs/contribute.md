# Contribute

The project is currently recording its baseline. Corrections to the 22 event
records, 13 contribution records, and 21 claim records are welcome.

New third-party mathematical submissions will not be listed until the project
has selected a content license and published its editorial charter. This keeps
the sequence, consent, attribution, and meaning of “listed” unambiguous.

- [Report a correction](https://github.com/nmonson1/guide-to-jacobian-conjecture/issues/new?template=correction.yml)
- [Improve a source](https://github.com/nmonson1/guide-to-jacobian-conjecture/issues/new?template=source.yml)
- [Inspect the repository](https://github.com/nmonson1/guide-to-jacobian-conjecture)

Listed does not mean endorsed. When intake opens, submission records, claims,
and assessments will remain separate objects, and a separate editorial
decision will be required before a claim changes the guide’s exposition.
