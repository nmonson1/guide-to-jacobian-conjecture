## JCG-C-0002 · A counterexample to the Jacobian conjecture

| Field | Record |

| Source date | 20 July 2026 |
| Origin | Historical import |

| Claims | `JCG-CLAIM-0002` through `JCG-CLAIM-0009` |

The imported artifact is the public note *A Counterexample to the Jacobian
Conjecture*. Its PDF has no visible author byline. Its introduction says that
the formula was announced by Levent Alpöge and that the announcement credits
Akhil Mathew for asking the question and Fable for the work leading to the
example. This project records that attribution without independently deciding
authorship or priority.
